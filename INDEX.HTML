Pro Tips

Exam Preparation Guide

Strategies to maximize your score in the Science board exam.

01

Understand, Don't Memorize

Concepts in Physics and Chemistry require deep understanding. Work through derivations and diagrams repeatedly until they feel natural.

02

Solve Last 10 Year Papers

Board exams follow patterns. Solving previous papers within timed conditions sharpens speed and reveals high-frequency topics.

03

Draw Diagrams Always

Labeled diagrams fetch bonus marks. Practice circuit diagrams, refraction diagrams, and biological diagrams to perfection.

04

Master 1-Mark & 2-Mark Questions

Short-answer questions carry heavy aggregate marks. Practice concise, keyword-rich answers — examiners look for specific terms.

05

Revise Chemical Equations

Write and balance all chemical equations from every chapter. They appear every year and are easy full marks if practiced.

06

Time Management During Exam

Spend ~50 mins on each section. Attempt easy questions first, then tackle 5-mark questions. Always leave 15 mins for revision.
